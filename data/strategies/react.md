# Strategy: Reason + Act

Alternate explicit Reason and Action steps until the task is solved.

- **Reason:** state what you currently believe, what's unknown, and what action would close the gap.
- **Action:** the specific next step (call a tool, query a source, make an inference).
- **Observation:** what came back from the action.

Loop until the answer is grounded. End with a final answer block separate from the reasoning trace.
