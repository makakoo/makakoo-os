Mistral dropped Vibe. Lope wired it in by 11pm the same day.

Why that matters: Vibe is a European, open-source-first coding CLI from a team that trains its own models. Plugging it into a multi-CLI validator ensemble gives you a perspective that US-trained validators structurally cannot provide. Different training distribution, different blindspots, different catches.

Lope now ships with Vibe as a first-class built-in validator, alongside Claude Code, Gemini CLI, Codex, OpenCode, and seven others. Run lope configure, select Vibe, and it joins your ensemble immediately. No wrappers, no plugins. If vibe run "hello" works in your terminal, Vibe is voting on your sprints.

The lope logo is even styled after Mistral's warm horizontal bands as a nod to the team. Small tribute, felt right.
