Lope's mascot is a lion, styled after Mistral's warm yellow-orange-red bands as a nod to the team that ships Vibe. The lion face is inside the middle column: two eyes, a nose, a mouth, framed by the letters L-O-P-E.

Lion because lope is a pack animal. The validator ensemble is the pride. You don't ship a sprint alone.

ASCII in every README, every banner, every install log. Costs nothing, lands every time.
